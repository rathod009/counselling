About ACE-AI

This platform offers comprehensive online career assessment, guidance, and counseling for students in India. Utilizing a multi-dimensional assessment that evaluates personality, aptitude, interests, general intelligence, and it helps users discover suitable career paths.

What Is Career Counselling?

Career counseling provides support and guidance for educational and career decisions, addressing confusion at any stage. Expert counselors assist in exploring career options, educational pathways, and opportunities.

What Are Career Assessments?

Career assessments analyze strengths and skills to recommend appropriate careers. This platform's assessment, tailored for the Indian context, offers a detailed report with career development plans to facilitate informed decision-making.
